ABOUT ME

Elvis Ma
is a teenage photographer and filmmaker based in Los Angeles, California. He began making films as soon as he had the ability to. At age 12, he made films on his iPhone 4 and learned video editing on his computer. At age 14, he learned how to use Photoshop and had created many videos with his friends. At age 16, he bought his first DSLR and learned video composite editing, drawing inspiration from Sam Kolder and Casey Neistat. Now, he is taking on freelancing jobs, mainly editing and photographing for Highschool friends.
